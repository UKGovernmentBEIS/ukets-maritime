Some principles are aspirational and you may not be able to confirm absolute compliance. Some are reliant on others being met before compliance can be confirmed.

Consistency, comparability and transparency of report

Monitoring and reporting need to be consistent and comparable over time. For that reason, Maritime operators must use the same monitoring methodologies and data sets, subject to changes and exemptions approved by the regulator.

Maritime operators need to obtain, record, compile, analyse and document monitoring data, including assumptions, references, activity data and calculation factors, in a transparent manner that enables the reproduction of the determination of emissions by the verifier and the regulator.
