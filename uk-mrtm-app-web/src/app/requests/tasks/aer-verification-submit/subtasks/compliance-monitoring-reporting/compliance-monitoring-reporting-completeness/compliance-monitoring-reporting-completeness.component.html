Some principles are aspirational and you may not be able to confirm absolute compliance. Some are reliant on others being met before compliance can be confirmed.

Monitoring and reporting must be complete and cover all voyages performed by the Maritime Operator that are defined as a maritime activity listed in Schedule TBD of the UKETS Order. The emissions data for the voyages must be complete.

Maritime Operators need to take appropriate measures to prevent any data gaps in the reporting period.
