Enter details of the procedures used to regularly check that the monitoring plan reflects the ships and processes used, and whether the monitoring methodology can be improved or needs revision.

You should include:

  • checks made to the list of emissions sources to ensure that monitoring remains complete and any changes to the nature or function of the ship are accounted for
  • assessing compliance with uncertainty values identified in the monitoring plan
  • evaluating potential improvements to the applied monitoring methodology
Simple procedures may be completely reflected by the description. However, when the procedure is more complex, it may be described in an external source (such as a written procedure or harmonised quality, environmental or energy management standards). If so, a brief description will suffice, with a reference to the full procedure and indicate where its full version is stored.
