What happens next

Your appointed verifier will be able to review activities within your account so they can prepare your Annual Emissions Report (AER).

The primary contact on your account will receive an email on 1 January each year giving notice that a task has appeared on the account dashboard to produce and submit an AER by 31 March.

When the AER has been submitted the verifier will receive a task on their dashboard to verify the report.

An operator admin user on your account can change verifiers by selecting the users and contacts option from the dashboard.
